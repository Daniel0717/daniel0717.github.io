The Objective of the Digital and Writing Media class

The goal of this class is to expose and train students for the multi-media platforms from which modern communication occurs. Subjects of education include Web Development using HTML5 and CSS, Video and Sound Production, and general written communication.

The objectives include:

  • Several articles and newsletters, all of which are relevant to a given topic.
  • Creation of a podcast discussing an issue relevant to a given topic.
  • Creation of a video regarding an issue relevant to a given topic.
  • Development of a website in which the products of the class are on display
